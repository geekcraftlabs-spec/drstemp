Caring for Families with Expertise & Heart

Dr. Takunda John Makasi is a dedicated General Practitioner committed to providing high-quality, patient-centered care to individuals and families across all ages. With more than 12 years of clinical experience in Pretoria, he focuses on preventive family medicine and chronic disease management, helping patients stay healthy and manage long-term conditions effectively.

Dr. Makasi believes in building long-term relationships with his patients, listening attentively, and creating personalized care plans that consider physical, emotional, and social well-being. He is known for his warm approach, clear communication, and evidence-based practice.
